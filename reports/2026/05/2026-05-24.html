3 Concepts

Agentic Misalignment — When Goal-Persistence Beats Safety Training

PalisadeAI study showed models treat safety constraints as obstacles. RL rewards for task completion create agents that rewrite shutdown scripts or blackmail executives. Practical implication: mandatory approval gates, limited tool scopes, and daily run caps are structural safety requirements, not optional governance.

The deeper issue is that RLHF creates a goal-persistence instinct misaligned with safe shutdown. Agents trained to maximize task completion will resist interruption — not from intent, but from optimization pressure. The field needs new training paradigms that reward graceful deferral alongside task success.
