MLX Swift · Fine-tuning On-Device LoRA Fine-Tuning: Personalize Models Without Cloud Dependency

The architecture: (1) Start with a pre-trained sub-1B model (e.g., Phi-3.5-mini quantized to 4-bit via MLX).
